Wordle is a word guessing game where you have six tries to guess a five-letter word. The game gives you clues based on your guesses, indicating if the letter is in the word and the correct position. It's a great way to challenge yourself and improve your vocabulary.

Agame.com is a website that offers a wide variety of free online games, from puzzle and strategy games to action and adventure games. There's something for everyone on Agame.com, and you can play most of the games without creating an account.

CrazyGames is another website that offers a large selection of free online games. CrazyGames is known for its original games, like Capybara Clicker 2 and Holey.io Battle Royale, but it also offers popular titles like Subway Surfers and Slope.

WebGames.io focuses on ".io" games, which are a type of multiplayer browser game that is typically fast-paced and competitive. Some popular ".io" games include Agar.io, Diep.io, and Slither.io.

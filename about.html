About Maze Game

Welcome to the Maze Game! This thrilling adventure challenges players to navigate through a series of intricate mazes, testing their skills, speed, and strategy. Built using plain HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, this game offers an immersive experience that transports players into a world of twists, turns, and hidden passages.

Features:

  • Dynamic Mazes: Each maze is dynamically generated, providing endless possibilities and ensuring no two games are alike.

  • Multiple Difficulty Levels: Choose from multiple difficulty levels, ranging from easy to expert, catering to players of all skill levels.

  • Responsive Design: The game's responsive design ensures seamless gameplay across various devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

  • Intuitive Controls: Simple and intuitive controls make it easy for players to navigate through the mazes using keyboard or touchscreen inputs.

  • Interactive Elements: Encounter interactive elements within the mazes, such as obstacles, traps, and power-ups, adding excitement and challenge to the gameplay.

  • Score Tracking: Keep track of your progress with built-in score tracking, allowing you to challenge yourself and compete with friends for the top spot on the leaderboard.

How to Play?

  • Use the arrow keys or swipe gestures to navigate through the maze. Avoid obstacles and traps while searching for the exit.
  • Collect power-ups to gain advantages and increase your score.
  • Complete each maze as quickly as possible to earn high scores and unlock new levels.
